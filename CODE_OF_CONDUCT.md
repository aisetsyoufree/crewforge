# Code of Conduct

Crew Forge is a technical beta project. Participation should stay constructive, specific, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and kind.
- Critique code, behavior, and product decisions rather than people.
- Share reproducible details when reporting issues.
- Respect privacy. Do not post secrets, credentials, private code, or private logs.
- Assume beta testers are working across different tools, operating systems, and skill levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Posting credentials, tokens, private code, or sensitive logs.
- Repeated off-topic comments after a maintainer asks to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or restrict participation to keep the project useful and safe for testers.

Report serious conduct issues privately through the maintainer contact channels listed in the repository.
